About Me

EXPERIENCE

Hi! I'm Alan, a 17-year-old full-stack developer at the North Carolina School of Science and Mathematics. I love building technology that creates real-world impact, especially in education and community-focused initiatives.

Some of my interests include machine learning, web development, cybersecurity but the common theme behind all of my projects is using technology to solve meaningful problems. I love turning code into something that helps others.

I'm always exploring new opportunities to learn, build, and collaborate on projects that can improve people's lives, even at the local-level!
